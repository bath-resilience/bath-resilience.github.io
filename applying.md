If you have an idea for an activity that you could deliver during the themed semester, there may be funding available to support this. [Please submit your idea via the form](https://forms.office.com/Pages/ResponsePage.aspx?id=Ij1-N6FOLUKwrY_MiUBrns648OV8E65Iu4iy7ZV6aHFUNlNSUDg5QlExT0JNSTQ2Qk9QVjVYOFUxUy4u).
 
The activity must be delivered in collaboration with someone from the Department of Mathematical Sciences or the Institute for Mathematical Innovation (IMI). If you have any questions, or want help identifying colleagues in Mathematical Sciences or IMI to work with you, please email <strong>{{ site.email }}</strong>.
